About Messo

Messo is a leading platform designed to help students find trusted and reliable accommodation across the country. Our platform connects students with verified hostels, ensuring a safe and convenient living experience. Our goal is to provide an affordable, accessible, and easy way for students to find the perfect place to call home.

Our Vision

To revolutionize student living by offering a network of trusted and comfortable hostels that cater to the unique needs of every student.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the student accommodation process by offering a seamless experience with a focus on safety, affordability, and accessibility for all students.

Our Core Values

  • Student-Centric: We always put students first in everything we do.
  • Trust: Building long-lasting relationships based on trust and integrity.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our platform and services to better meet the needs of students.
  • Collaboration: Working together with educational institutions and accommodation providers to create a better living experience for students.
